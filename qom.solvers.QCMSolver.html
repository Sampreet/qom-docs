class qom.solvers.QCMSolver.QCMSolver(modes, corrs)¶

Bases: object

Class to handle quantum correlation measure solver.

Initializes corrs and modes.

Parameters:

  • modes (list) – Classical mode amplitudes of the system.

  • corrs (list) – Quantum correlations of the quadratures.
